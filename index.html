Tom's Sports Day Challenge

Tom's school organized its annual sports day last Friday. The event included various competitions, such as running races, long jump, high jump, and relays.

Tom signed up for both the 100-meter sprint and the long jump, as he wanted to challenge himself and represent his class well.

In the week leading up to the event, Tom trained intensely. Every evening, he practiced his sprinting form and worked on his jump technique, even though he felt exhausted after school. He knew that preparation was key to performing well.

On the big day, Tom arrived early and warmed up with his teammates. During the 100-meter sprint, he pushed himself as hard as he could and finished with one of his best times, though not enough to win.

Later, in the long jump event, he managed a solid leap that placed him in the top three.

Although he didn't take home a gold medal, Tom felt satisfied. He had improved, pushed his limits, and learned that consistent effort matters more than just winning. He promised himself to train smarter and return stronger next year.
